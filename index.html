Welcome on the Postfly business card creator site
On this site you can you design your own business card step by step.
It works as follows:
  1. Choose 1 out of 3 available business card sizes.
  2. Choose your printing method, 4/0 CMYK means printed on just one side, 4/4 CMYK means printed double sided.
  3. Choose 1 of the available background colors.
  4. Select your paper type, if you are not familiar with our paper types or you want to physically see the paper types, please request the sample kit by clicking the button below.
Sample kit
  1. Select the quantity you would like.
  2. With the remaining steps you are able to personalize your card, you can:
    • • Upload your own photo or logo.
    • • You can add one or more text fields for your name / address / email / website / phone number, etc.

You will always see a preview of your design. If you are satisfied with your design, enter your name and e-mail address and hit the request quotation button. You are also able to download the preview of your design or start over by clicking the reset button.
Would you like to start your design journey? Then please click the start designing button.
