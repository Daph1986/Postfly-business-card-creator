Welcome to the Postfly business card creator
On this website you can design your own business card step by step.
It works as follows:
  • 1. Choose 1 out of 3 available business card sizes.
  • 2. Choose the printing method. 4/0 CMYK means printed on just one side, 4/4 CMYK means printed double sided.
  • 3. Choose 1 of the 8 available background colors.
  • 4. Select the paper type. If you are not familiar with our paper types or you want to physically see the paper types, please request a sample kit by clicking the button below.
Sample kit
  • 5. Select the quantity you would like to order.
  • 6. With the remaining steps you are able to personalize your card, you can:
    • • Upload your own photo or logo.
    • • You can add one or more text fields for your name / address / email / website / phone number, etc.

You will see a preview of your design as you create your card. If you are satisfied with it, enter your name and e-mail address and click on the send button to request your quotation. You can also download a sample of your design or start over by clicking the reset button.
